Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about college comparison, cutoffs, and budgets

How does Gettn Ngo calculate college admission odds?
Our system compares your board or state exam marks (TS/AP EAPCET) against the previous three years of official cutoff database entries. It takes into account your specific subject interests, category limits, and current year applicant ratios.
Is Gettn Ngo completely free for students?
Yes, Gettn Ngo is an open, student-first platform designed to democratize higher education planning. All discovery filters, scholarship searches, side-by-side comparison matrices, and cutoffs are 100% free with no hidden charges.
Can I upload my own colleges list via CSV?
Absolutely! Once you sign in or enter Guest Mode, you'll have access to the Data Import Portal. You can download our standardized CSV template, populate it with institute codes, courses, fees, and seats, and drop it in to instantly update your local database!
How reliable are the placement packages and accreditation details shown?
All academic and placement metrics shown are synced with government databases, NIRF reports, and official college reports verified by state university management systems (OU, JNTUH, AU). Each card displays official trust badges like NBA and NAAC.
